Retirement Fund Distributions

Enter income you execpt to generate by selling a portion of your retirements funds, and making deposits into your checking account.

401k plan distributions are fully taxable since the contributions were excluded from your taxable income.

Traditional IRA distributions are subject to personal income taxes on any gains, and on any tax deferred contributions.

Roth IRA distributions are not subject to personal income taxes as long as your first Roth IRA contribution was made at least five years prior to any distribution, and the funds are distributed after you reach age 59 and a half.
