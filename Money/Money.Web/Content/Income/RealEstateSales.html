Real Estate Sales

Enter income generated by the sale of a home, condo, or land that you own.

Income from real estate sales is subject to capital gain taxes (minus your exemption for the sale of your main home), which we will estimate and track for you.  Up to $250,000 in capital gains is excluded from selling a main home ($500,000 if married), as long as you have lived in the home for a least 2 or the 5 years prior to selling the home.
