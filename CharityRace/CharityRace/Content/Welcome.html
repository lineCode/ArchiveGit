Welcome to the Happy Face Road Race!

The Happy Face Road Race raises money for life-saving cancer research and treatment at the Regional Cancer Institute through an annual bike-a-thon that crosses the state.

Since its founding in 1980, the HFRR has successfully melded support from committed cyclists, volunteers, corporate sponsors and individual contributors. All are essential to the HFRR's goal and model: to attain maximum fundraising efficiency while increasing its annual gift.

Our hope and aspiration is to provide the doctors and researchers the necessary resources to discover cures for all cancers.
