The Project Browser

The Project Browser appears whenever you choose to create a new project using a Ready-Made design, or when you want to open an existing project.

The lists on the left side of the window organize the ready-made projects into groups with similar characteristics.

  • Click on a list box to reveal the choices in the list.

  • Click on a description in the list to "sort" through the entire list and display only the projects that fit the description.

  • Choose one description in a list, or choose "All" if you want more than one in the same list.

  • Click the scroll bar at the right of the screen until you find one that you like.

  • Click on a project, then click the Select button to open it in the Design Workspace.

Note: To find the project you want, you can also type a couple of descriptive words in the "Keywords" box, then click the Find Now button.
