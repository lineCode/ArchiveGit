Online Art Gallery

PrintMaster's Online Art Gallery is the simple way to obtain additional pictures for your projects without having to switch CD-ROMs. Just by selecting the Online Art Gallery images in the Art Gallery, PrintMaster will connect to the Internet, download the image, and place it in your project.

To add an Online Art Gallery picture to your project and Online Collection:

  1. Double-click a preview in the Art Gallery labeled 'Online Art Gallery Collection.' Only the preview is provided in your copy of PrintMaster. You will have to download the actual picture file from the Online Art Gallery to use it in your project or store it in the Online Collection.

  2. If this is the first time you have connected to the Online Art Gallery, you will be asked to create a new account. Insert a Name, Password, and Email Address in the dialog boxes. Please write down the information you provided so you will have it in case you need it (e.g. you get a new computer and want to re-establish your account.)

  3. If you are already connected to the Internet (for example, if you already launched your online service or have a direct connection to the Internet), PrintMaster will access the picture you selected and download it into your Online Collection. If you are not connected to the Internet, PrintMaster will launch your Internet service first, then download the picture file.

  4. After the picture file has been downloaded, you will notice the preview in the Art Gallery no longer says 'Online Art Gallery'. That means the picture file is now copied into your project and onto your hard drive in the Online Collection. You will not have to download it again to use the picture in your projects.
