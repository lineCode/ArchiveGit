Mail Merge Fields

Mail Merge fields are placeholders that can be "filled-in" with personal information from your Address Book. This allows you to address your card, envelope, or other project to more than one person, and you won't need to change the text for each one.

To add a Mail Merge field to your project:

  1. Begin by adding a text box to your label project. If you are working with a Ready Made project, delete the sample text in the text box, but leave the empty box.

  2. Make sure the empty text box is selected (the handles are showing around it), and the insertion point (the blinking cursor) is placed inside the text box.

NOTE: Mail Merge fields do not have to be inserted into an empty text box. They can also be added to text boxes that already contain existing text. Mail Merge fields are inserted at the text cursor position.

  1. Under the Add menu, select Mail Merge Field.... A list of available Mail Merge fields will then appear. Use the scroll bar to the right to move up or down the list of fields.

  2. Pick a Mail Merge field by clicking on its name.

  3. Click the Insert button to add the selected field to your project page.

    Or, to more easily insert standard information:

    • Click Receiver Address to automatically insert Receiver First Name, Last Name, Title, Company, Address1, Address2, City, State, and Zip fields. This is often the best selection when you are sending out lots of mailers, envelopes, or invitations with a different name and address on each item.

    • Click Sender Address to automatically insert the Sender First Name, Last Name, Title, Company, Address1, Address2, City, State, and Zip fields. This will create a series of labels with your name and address on each. This is best when you want to create many identical return address labels, cassette labels, books plates, or business cards.
