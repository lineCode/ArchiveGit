Advanced Print Settings

Clicking the Advanced... button in the Print dialog box displays the Advanced Settings dialog.

Outline Contrast:

Use the Outline Picture Contrast slider to adjust the lightness or darkness of printed outline images such as CGM and WMF graphics. Use the Bitmap Picture Contrast slider to adjust the lightness or darkness of printed bitmap images such as graphics in the TIF, PCX or BMP formats.

Picture Contrast:

Picture Contrast does not effect text or other items such as Calendar lines. If images are printing too dark, adjust the slider to the left, towards the word Light. If images are printing too light, adjust the slider to the right, towards the word Dark. A setting of 1000 will cause PrintMaster to send images to the printer without any contrast adjustments.

Note: Dot matrix and HP DeskJet printers can sometimes print too dark or too light. Use the Picture Contrast controls to lighten or darken the printout.
