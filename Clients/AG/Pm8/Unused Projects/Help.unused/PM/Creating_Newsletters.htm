CREATING NEW DOCUMENTS:

 
Creating Newsletters

Use the Newsletter project to create newsletters, bulletins, newspapers, and other multi-page documents. You choose the style and number of columns, then add your own content and graphics. PrintMaster includes several styles of newsletters to choose from - from personal to professional.

Newsletter projects use specially connected text boxes that allow text to flow from column to column, and they allow you to add and remove pages quickly and easily. In every other way, they are no different from a simple greeting card project.

Modify one of the ready-made newsletters to get started quickly. The PrintMaster user manual also contains a tutorial on creating a newsletter project.
