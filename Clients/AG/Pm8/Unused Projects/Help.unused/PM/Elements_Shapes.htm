Elements: Shapes

Click on the Shape tool or the Shape... command in the Add menu to view a wide selection of shapes that you can place on your project.

 
Once you select a shape from the pop-up menu, drag your cursor across the project to determine its size. You can maintain the original proportions of the shape by holding down the Shift key while you drag.
