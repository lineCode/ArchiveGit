DESIGN BASICS:

Adding Design Elements:

The Edit Toolbar

Add elements to your project from the Add menu in the Design Workspace, or from the Edit toolbar. After you select an element for the project, it appears in the workspace.

The Edit toolbar
The Edit toolbar contains the tools for adding the following types of elements to your project:

Images
Choose from the vast array of art included with PrintMaster, order online art, or add your own images.

 

Sentiments
Choose from hundreds of quotes and phrases that help you find just the right words.

 

Cartoon-O-Matic images
Create a customized cartoon face with this add-on application.

 

DrawPlus images
DrawPlus is an award-winning drawing, graphics, and illustration program included with PrintMaster.

 

BorderPlus borders
Add emphasis to your projects with BorderPlus--an easy-to-use add on to PrintMaster that helps you create customized borders for any project.

 

 Tables
A table is a small grid that can be placed on your project for arranging many small bits of text information or aligning several small pictures.

 

Text boxes
Add your own words, and choose from many custom effects.

 

Lines, Rectangles and Ellipses(ovals)
Use these regular shapes to add emphasis, separate items, etc.

 

Shapes
Choose from a wide variety of symbols and design shapes.

 

Free Drawn Lines
Just like using a pencil or paintbrush on the screen.

 

Note: To hide or display the Edit toolbar, click the Toolbars command in the View menu, then click on the 'Edit Bar'. Check marks show which toolbars will appear on screen. Follow this procedure to display or hide any toolbar.
