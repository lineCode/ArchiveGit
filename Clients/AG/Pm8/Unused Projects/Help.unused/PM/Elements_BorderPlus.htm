DESIGN BASICS:

Adding Design Elements:
Elements: BorderPlus

BorderPlus is an easy-to-use add on to PrintMaster that helps you create customized borders for any project.

 

To add a border to your project:

  1. Click the BorderPlus! icon in the Workspace.

  2. Drag the text tool across your label to create the outside dimensions of your border. When you release the mouse, BorderPlus! will appear and show you a blank border of the size you created in its window.

  3. There are eight 'panels' to each border: the top, bottom, left, and right sides, and the four corners. When you open BorderPlus!, all eight panels are selected.

NOTE: When you import a picture to fill one of the panels, the panel must first be selected. To select or deselect a panel, click on it once.

  1. Click the Art Gallery button to open the Art Gallery. Select a picture to be repeated in your border panel, or use Import From Disk to bring in any other picture you might want to use.

  2. Adjust the width of the different border panels by dragging the inside edge of the border with your mouse.

  3. When you are finished with your border, click the Close button, or select Done Editing Border... from the File menu. Your border will be placed on your project. You can now move or resize it as you would any picture object.
