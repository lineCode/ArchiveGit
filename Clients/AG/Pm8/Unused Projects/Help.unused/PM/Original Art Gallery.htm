Original Art Gallery

PrintMaster includes an exclusive collection of Original Art. Created by internationally known painters, designers, and illustrators, the images cover every theme and occasion you can think of, so it's easy to find just the right one. Just type "Original" as the keyword in PrintMaster's Image Browser and you can view the entire collection.

To browse through this exclusive collection:

  1. Open the Art Gallery by clicking the Art Gallery button in the Edit toolbar, the 'Picture' command in the Add menu, or clicking the Art Gallery button in the Hub screen.

  2. Type the word "Original" in the box labeled 'Keyword'.

  3. Click the Find button.

    The Original Art Gallery collection will appear in the preview window of the Art Gallery.
