Working With Projects:

 

Saving your Project

You can save projects as individual files and as a group of related files. For example, you might group your company’s business cards, envelopes and letterhead together in the same collection.

Saving your project:

  1. Click the Save command in the File menu of the Design Workspace.

  2. Enter a name for your project.

  3. Select the options as outlined below.

    Save copy of picture files in project
    This option helps you coordinate sets of documents that use the same images. For example if your company logo appears on your letterhead, business card and envelope, leave this option "unchecked" so that if you change your logo, it will change in all the documents where it appears.

    Category:
    PrintMaster also lets you save your projects into your own groups in the Project Gallery--called "categories". When you save a project for the first time, or click the Save As command in the File menu of the Design Workspace, you'll see this option at the bottom of the dialog box. Choose one of your existing categories from the list, or click the New... button to create a new category.

  4. Click the Save button.
