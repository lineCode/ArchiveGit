ELEMENTS: Setting Position and Size

When you need to precisely specify the Size or Position of an element in your project, click the More... command in the Arrange > Position submenu, the popup menu, or the status bar popup.

The dialog box that appears lets you select from several pre-assigned measurements and percentages, or you can enter your own value. The Preview displayed at the right of the box shows what effect your changes will have.

This feature could be helpful if you wanted to use preprinted letterhead containing a design or logo, or to fit a graphic on a specific size label, etc.
