Elements: Lines, Rectangles and Ellipses

Lines, rectangles, and ellipses are graphics that you can create in your projects. These elements are simple geometric shapes that you can create in any size shape color and pattern.

Click this tool to draw a straight line on your project. To keep your line either horizontal, perpendicular, or at a 45-degree angle, hold down the Shift key while you drag the cursor across your project.

 

Use this tool to create a rectangle on your project. Hold down the Shift key, and your box will always be a perfect square.

 

Use this tool to create an ellipse. Hold down the Shift key, and your ellipse will always be a perfect circle.
