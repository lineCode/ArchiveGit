PrintMaster on the Internet...

When you click on the Internet button, PrintMaster will search for your Internet browser and launch it. After the browser opens, follow the on-screen instructions to reach the PrintMaster Web site.

With an Internet connection and a Web browser, you can access the PrintMaster Web site to get more information on the new upgrades, late-breaking news, download additional graphics, trade design tips with other PrintMaster users, and more!

Getting Connected:
If your don't have an Internet connection, all you need is a modem on your computer, and PrintMaster makes it easy to get on the Internet. PrintMaster includes an easy setup kit for AT&T WorldNet™ that will configure your computer for Internet access, and install the Netscape Navigator™ Internet browser software – everything you’ll need to get on the Internet.

  • Open the PrintMaster program group in Windows and click the Setup AT&T WorldNet icon. Follow the directions to get online fast!

PrintMaster’s Online Greetings:

With PrintMaster’s Online Greetings you can send the Greeting Cards you design to the PrintMaster web site for your friends or family to see. You enter your Online Greeting information, we’ll send an e-mail notification to both you and your receiver, letting you both know it’s ready.

To send a greeting card to your friends and family using PrintMaster’s Online Greetings:

  1. Create your card and save it as usual.

  2. Launch your Internet Browser to establish an Internet connection.

  3. From the PrintMaster Design Workspace, go to the File menu and select Send Online Greeting...

  4. Enter your e-mail address and the e-mail address of your recipient.

  5. Click OK.

    PrintMaster sends your greeting card to the Web site. You and the recipient will receive an e-mail notification letting you know when the card is available for viewing. The recipient’s e-mail notification includes all the instructions needed to access the site and view the card.
