Creating a Brand New Project

Clicking the Brand New button in the Hub screen opens the New Project screen -- a small window (known as a "wizard") where you will choose the type of project and answer a question or two about the project you wish to create.

After making your selections, the design window appears, and you're ready to work on your project. PrintMaster creates the document you see on the screen from the selections you made.
