The Design Workspace

The PrintMaster Design "Workspace"
The workspace is the screen that appears after you open an existing project or create a new project. It is the area where you do the work of making your project.

The screen has a central area where your project appears, surrounded by toolbars for adding and modifying the elements in your project. The workspace is where it all comes together... it's your high-tech easel - complete with a host of powerful tools.
