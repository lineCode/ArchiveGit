Year You Were Born

The Year You Were Born feature in PrintMaster is a fun way to include historical information on your projects that's tied to your birth date, or the birth date of your friends.
 

To insert text information from the Year You Were Born:

  1. Click the Year You Were Born button. You will then see the Year You Were Born dialog. Select any of the topics you would like to include in your Year You Were Born report. To include the your name and date information at the top, be sure to check the selection labelled "Introduction" .

  2. Click OK. The Year You Were Born program will search through its database for information that matches your date and topic requests. The corresponding report will appear in text boxes on your project. You can format and move the text boxes as you would in any other text box.
