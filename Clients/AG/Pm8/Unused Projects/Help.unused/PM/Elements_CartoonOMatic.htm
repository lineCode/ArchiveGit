Adding Design Elements:
Elements: Cartoon-O-Matic

Cartoon-O-Matic is a fun way to create your own pictures. This add-on program gives you several selections of cartoon art, which you can modify to your specific tastes. When it’s just right, click OK and the picture appears in your project.

(Click on the picture below for more information about Cartoon-o-Matic.)

Facial Parts Buttons
Located just to the right of the preview window, these buttons let you select the part of the face to modify (nose, eyes, ears,etc.).

Slider Controls
Clicking on the facial parts buttons displays different slider controls - in the area just below the buttons - for that part of the face. These sliders let you adjust the level for the facial characteristic. Click and drag the slider, and watch the face change!

New Button
Click this button to change to a different face. A preview window appears, and you click the arrows beneath to pick a different cartoon face.

Zoom Control and Random Button
The zoom slider control is just below the face, and it makes the face bigger or smaller. The random button (the single die) arranges all the facial parts at random – try it and see!
