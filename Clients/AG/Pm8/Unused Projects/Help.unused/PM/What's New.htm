What's New in Version 7.0

DrawPlus
PrintMaster now includes a powerful, full-featured drawing package for creating and editing images, and producing special text effects that make your projects even more unique. Click on the DrawPlus button (located on the toolbar on the left side of the workspace) to check out this great new feature!

Web Page Publishing
Develop your own Web page with PrintMaster's fun and easy web publishing tools. You get special Web art, animated GIF files, and ready-to-print templates, as well as a design checker. (Note: Web Page Publishing is not available in PrintMaster Classic.)

BorderPlus
A terrific way to decorate invitations and special awards! Create your own unique borders with thousands of images, photos, and original art.

Year You Were Born
Find out what was happening in the world during the year you were born. Insert fun facts into birthday cards and party favors and personalize them even more! Just click on the Year You Were Born button located on the left toolbar in the workspace.

More Calendar Features
Create yearly calendars in a variety of different formats. You also get more ready-to-print calendar layouts, and greater flexibility in placing images.

Craft Corner
PrintMaster has templates for all sorts of crafts, including 3D projects like gift bags, baskets, paper airplanes-even entire towns and farms. (Note: 3D projects also require scissors and glue.)

Fun For Kids
Check out "Fun For Kids" in the Project Gallery. Includes ready-to-print masks, hand puppets, party hats, crazy stickers, cool bookmarks. (Note: Not all projects are available in all versions of PrintMaster.)

An Enhanced Help System
PrintMaster now includes context-sensitive Helpful Hints with useful tips, Mini Tutorials for step-by-step learning, and Design Tutorials for design ideas. You'll also receive helpful Audio Tips in each section of the program. (Note: Sound card and speakers required).

Online Art Gallery™
An exclusive new feature only for PrintMaster users! Now you can directly access thousands of FREE images while you're still working in PrintMaster. (Note: User must have access to the Internet.)
