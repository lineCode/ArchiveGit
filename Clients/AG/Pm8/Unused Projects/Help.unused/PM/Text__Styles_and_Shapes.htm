Adding Design Elements:

Text-Styles and shapes

PrintMaster supports any fonts installed on your computer. As with many word processing programs, PrintMaster also supports changing font sizes, styles and alignments. Use the Text Toolbar to change the style of any text you select.

Selecting Text:

As a rule, select the text that you want to modify before clicking the buttons to change the style or shape.

To select text:

  1. Click the mouse at the beginning of the area of text you want to modify. A blinking line appears where you clicked called the “cursor”.

  2. Click and hold the mouse button down, and move your mouse over the rest of the text you want to modify. The text you select reverses color. The text is now “highlighted”.

  3. Use the Text toolbar or the commands in the Format menu to modify the text.

Modifying Text:

  • You can also use the Text toolbar to pre-set the style and shape of text you are about to type.

  • Changes to Text Shape, Stretching and Alignment apply to the entire text box or paragraph of text.

  • The Format menu contains the same commands as the Text toolbar, and other advanced features for modifying text.

  • Clicking the right mouse button inside a selected text box displays a small menu of text-related commands known as a “popup” menu.
