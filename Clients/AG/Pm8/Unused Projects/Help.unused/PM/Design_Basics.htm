Designing Projects

Designing projects can be as simple or complicated as you make it. PrintMaster can help you find the shortest distance between your idea and reality, or you can take the "scenic" route, and create top-quality designs from the ground up.

The PrintMaster User's Guide contains suggestions to help you polish your design skills, and tutorials to help you get familiar with creating a basic project from start to finish.
