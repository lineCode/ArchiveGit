Using the Art Gallery

The Art Gallery appears whenever you choose to add an image to a project. The Art Gallery contains thousands of drawings, photos, borders, and paintings, many created specially for PrintMaster.

The lists on the left side of the window organize the multitude of project-ready art into groups with similar characteristics.

  • Click on a list box to reveal the choices in the list.

  • Clicking on a description in the list "sorts" through the entire list and displays only the projects that fit the description.

  • Choose one description in a list, or choose "All" if you want more than one in the same list.

  • Once you've narrowed down the number of choices, click the scroll bar on the right side of the screen until you find one that you like.

Special groups of images:
To find the image you want, you can type a couple of descriptive words in the "Keywords" box, then click the Find button.

PrintMaster includes three special groups of images accessible from the Art Gallery:

  • Original Art: These images were designed exclusively for PrintMaster users. You won't find them anywhere else. Just type the word "Original" in the Keyword field, then click the Find button to see the collection.

  • American Greetings® Collection: These images from American Greetings® are perfect for creating cards, invitations and adding just the right touch to your project. Just click the American Greetings® logo button in the Art Gallery to see the collection.

  • Royalty-free images: Type "EULA" in the Keyword field, then click the Find button to see a collection of images that can be used for commercial purposes - in accordance with the End User License Agreement (EULA).

NOTE: PrintMaster supports many different types of graphic formats, including CGM, TIFF, PSD, JPG, PCX, GIF, WMF, BMP, and Kodak Photo CD.
