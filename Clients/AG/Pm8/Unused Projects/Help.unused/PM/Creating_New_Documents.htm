CREATING NEW DOCUMENTS:

 
Creating New Documents

In PrintMaster, there are three ways to begin creating a new document:

  1. Choose one of the many Ready-Made professional documents.

  2. Create the project "with help" from the Design Wizard to get you started.

  3. Create a Brand New document on your own.

You can begin creating your document from almost any place in PrintMaster. Each of the methods above corresponds to a button in the “Hub” or opening screen, and also to a button in the “global” toolbar of the Design Workspace.
