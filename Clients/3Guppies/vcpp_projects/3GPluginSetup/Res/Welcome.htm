The 3Guppies Mobilizer browser and shell extension lets you easily send your pictures, video, audio files and text from your desktop or the web to most cell phones in the US.

Now that the Mobilizer is installed:

  • You can use your favorite browser to send content from any web page to your phone.
  • You can send files to your phone directly from any file on your desktop, hard disk, photo SD card, or USB drive.

To get started:

  1. Right-click on a web page background, a web page picture, or a media file anywhere on your computer.
  2. Select one of the 3Guppies menu extensions: Mobilize This!, Mobilize All!, or Mobilize Video!
  3. Once directed to the 3Guppies site, tell us what mobile phone number(s) you want to receive the media, and we will automatically re-format and deliver the media to those cell phone(s).

Select Mobilize This! to send a particular media file.

Select Mobilize All! to choose from all the media files on a web page.

In cases where video media is embedded in a web page you can Mobilize the video in one of two ways:

  • When using Firefox, hover over the video and a Mobilize This! link will appear above the media.
  • When Using Microsoft Internet Explorer, right-click anywhere on the page and select the Mobilize Video! menu item

The 3Guppies Mobilizer browser extension, and conversion and delivery service, works with just about any wireless carrier in the U.S. and are FREE.  Please note, however, that wireless carriers may charge extra for text messaging (SMS or MMS) or accessing the Internet.

So give it a try!  Now you can mobilize your media from wherever it lives.
